What's it all about?

The Tommy Vine Fund was set up in March 2013 as a legacy to the astonishing actor and truly beautiful and inspiring human being that was Tom Hare aka Tommy Vine.

We started by selling posters, poetry books, playlists...performed, made hundreds of badges, ran The Color Run and began spreading the wisdom of 'DON'T THINK JUST LET IT FLOW!!!'

What do we do?

We hold events with the purpose of showcasing the talents of those that may not otherwise have the opportunity to show them off!

We give away bursaries in order to aid creatives to go forth with their projects. This could be playwrights who need a cash injection to put on their play, young filmmakers, bands who would like to record their tracks....whoever!

Provide a support system to young creatives and encourage those with the arts less readily available to them to also follow their dreams.

We recently joined up with Ideas Tap and NSDF to give away two bursaries; one to a young producer specialising in interactive performances and another to a young animator whose short film explores the life of a lonely girl in London.

What's next...

In March 2015 we will return with our second 'The Flow Show' and give up and coming designers the opportunity to show off their collection

And don't forget our selection of Tommy T's for sale!

We are always scheming away to organise many more beautiful events - and we always love to hear from you!
